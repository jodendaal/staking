What is Cardano

Cardano is a proof-of-stake (POS) blockchain platform: the first to be founded on peer-reviewed research, developed through evidence-based methods. It combines pioneering technologies to provide unparalleled security and sustainability to decentralized applications, systems, and societies. Besides, with more than 1700 stakepools, Cardano is the most decentralized blockchain of the world. With a leading team of engineers, Cardano exists to redistribute power from unaccountable structures to the margins – to individuals – and be an enabling force for positive change and progress.

FAQ

WHAT ARE THE RISKS?

Staking your cardano is 100% safe, your funds are always in your control and do not leave your wallet. You should never send your ADA to someone in order to stake.

WHAT HAPPENS IF I ADD MORE ADA TO MY WALLET?

When you add ADA to your existing wallet, it is then automatically placed into the pool you are currently delegating to ZACS, and rewards will be calculated.

DO I NEED TO CLAIM MY REWARDS?

With Cardano and ZACS you never need to claim your rewards. Rewards are automatically deposited into your wallet every 5 days (epoch).

WHAT PERCENTAGE OF RETURN ON STAKE SHOULD I EXPECT?

Cardano.org states a 4.6% return on stake. Although currently 5% to 5.5% is common in the industry. Rewards may vary per epoch.

WHEN WILL I START EARNING REWARDS?

It takes four epochs before you start earning rewards. This can take 20-25 days, and will continue to pay you (even if you leave) for all epochs you participated in. One of the many benefits of Cardano.

CAN I LOSE ADA WHILE STAKING?

Your staked ADA is completely safe! Your staked funds remain solely in your control - stake pools never have access to your funds or rewards. Additionally, you can never lose funds by staking them.
